Fibernavigator

Welcome to the Fibernavigator's Github page!

The Fibernavigator is a tool designed for a fast and versatile visualization of fibers datasets. It is currently mainly developed by members of the Sherbrooke Connectivity Imaging Lab.

NEW! New tutorials of the Fibernavigator were created.

  • Intuitive 3D navigation
  • Mesh creation and interaction, as well as fusion of scalar volumes (T1, FA, etc.)
  • Drawing and segmentation tools
  • Tractogram and fibers visualization and interaction
To see the list of all tutorials, you can visit the Video tutorials page on the wiki of the project.
